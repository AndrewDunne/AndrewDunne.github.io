Wildlife Restoration Survey App

WIP project designed to help gauge people's preference on different types of environmental heterogeneity, such as clustering of trees, amount of woody debris, or presence of water.
The goal is to determine how to maximize both ecological benefit and human appreciation of wildlife restoration projects.

On the technical side, it uses a custom coded method of spawning objects and performing actions like clustering based on slider values.

Brook Constantz is the primary researcher, I've assisted with planning and developing the application in Unreal 4. All work shown in video by me.

Interpretive Work

"Seeing Colors" Art Analysis Blog

A blog where I look at popular digital art and try to discover how it was made, and what makes it good. The goal is to answer these difficult questions: 'What makes art good?' and 'How do skilled digital artists do their craft?'. The site serves as a place to teach people who don't have as much experience in digital art how to analyze their own artwork and other's, because not everyone has access to a good art teacher.
